Research

May, 2025
I am primarily interested in differential topology, with a focus on the existence of special geometric structures on smooth manifolds. In my dissertation, I proved that any smooth manifold of dimension at most 10, without compact components, admits a complex analytic structure if and only if it admits an almost complex structure. Moreover, I showed that any almost complex structure in this setting is homotopic to an integrable one.
